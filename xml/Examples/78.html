ܐܘܪܗܝ - Edessa

Edessa ‏‎ (1, 2, 5, 6) Urfa ‏‎ (1) ܐܘܪܗܳܝ ‏‎ (3, 5) الرها ‏‎ (4)

Place Type: city

Confessions: Syrian Orthodox (0451 - 1924) (1) , Muslim (0641 - ) (1) , Latin (1098 - 1144) (1)

Abstract

A city of Mesopotamia, the capital of the ancient kingdom of Osrhoene, modern Urfa.
